Work experience.

Jul 2018 @ Beyon3D

In 2018 I worked for a few weeks as an intern in Beyon3D, a high-tech startup company based in hertzelia-pituach, israel. I worked alongside professionals with years of engineering experience exposing me to many different disciplines within the single workspace.

My job was to fully automate a piece of industrial equipment as well as help with the main project of the company giving me experience in the Mechanical, Electrical, Computational and Integration sides of R&D.

This has given me a taste of life as an engineer in a high-tech startup, working long hours, collaborating on projects between different disciplines, the challenges of a professional workspace. This was an incredible experience, encouraging me to work in high-tech in the future.

Jan 2013 - Jul 2018 @ Hill House Equine Ltd.

Up to and during my IGCSE school years I was a professional international showjumper. Starting with some local competitions with 1.00m tall fences, advancing to 1.45m grand prix classes at venues all over Europe.

My greatest success during these years was towards the end of my career, in the 'Longines Global Champions Tour' in Cannes and Paris, winning the Grand Prix classes where Paris was my very last competition.

What I gained from this experience the most is commitment, resilience and teamwork. Through the difficult times is when I learned to keep pushing as a team, as the contribution and commitment of each member is crucial in order to flourish.

Doing this prohibited me from having a normal education, so inevitably I studied for and completed my IGCSE's independently while touring. I ultimately decided to stop in the months approaching my A-Level years to return to school to persue a more academic life path.
